Sonic The Hedgehog

The fastest thing alive

Here is a list of the first few mainline Sonic the Hedgehog games:

  • Sonic the Hedgehog – 1991: The first game in the series. It finds our protagonist saving his animal friends that have been roboticized by Robotnik.

  • Sonic the Hedgehog 2 – 1992: Sonic has to chase after Robotnik who is trying to get all the emeralds to rule the world. Tails, Sonic's sidekick, makes his first appearance here.

  • Sonic CD – 1992: In Sonic CD, Sonic has to travel through time to save Little Planet from the clutches of Dr. Robotnik. His memorable race with his look-a-like, Metal Sonic, has stayed through the years with many.

  • Sonic the Hedgehog Chaos – 1993: A game for the handheld system Game Gear. Sonic has to get back the emeralds Dr. Robotnik stole to create nuclear weapons.

  • Sonic the Hedgehog 3 – 1994: The first part of a 2 game epic adventure. Sonic has to race through the zones to prevent Dr. Robotnik from launching the Death Egg, a weaponized star.

  • Sonic & Knuckles – 1994: The second part of a 2 game epic adventure. After successfully prevent the launch of the Death Egg, Dr. Robotnik steals the Master Emerald by tricking Knuckles and successfully manages to send the star to orbit. An out of the atmosphere fight begins, between the super powered forces of good and evil.

  • Sonic the Hedgehog: Triple Trouble – 1994: The player controls either Sonic the Hedgehog or Miles "Tails" Prower as they venture to protect the powerful Chaos Emeralds from Doctor Robotnik, Knuckles the Echidna, and series newcomer Nack the Weasel.

  • Sonic Adventure - 1998: Sonic makes his debut into the 3d world in Sonic Adventure. Take control of Sonic, Tails, Knuckles, Amy, Big or Gamma. Protect Station Square from Dr. Robotnik and the new lifeform he discovered and is trying to use as a weapon, Chaos.

  • Sonic Adventure 2 – 2001: Sonic Adventure 2 features two good-vs-evil stories: Sonic the Hedgehog, Miles "Tails" Prower, and Knuckles the Echidna attempt to save the world, while Shadow the Hedgehog, Doctor Eggman, and Rouge the Bat attempt to conquer it.
